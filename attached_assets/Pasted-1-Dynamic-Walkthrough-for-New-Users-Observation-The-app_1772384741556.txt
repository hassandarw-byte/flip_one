Why It Matters:
A System Theme option enhances user comfort by providing a consistent experience across apps, adhering to users' device preferences. This improves usability and overall user satisfaction.
Possible Enhancements:
● Implement System Theme Detection: Add functionality to detect the device's current theme setting and automatically adapt the app's theme accordingly.
